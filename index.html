About Us

Dracarys was founded in 2021 by a group of university students from the University of Bedfordshire and SLIIT. The dracarys had grown into a vast crew that had completed about 500 projects all around the world.

We are mostly interested in design and marketing, but we have also worked on web and app development. The biggest advantage of working with us is that we have people all around the world who have various and unique ideas, as well as some outstanding Sri Lankan designers. We are always improving our technology in order to provide you with the greatest ideas that will benefit you.
